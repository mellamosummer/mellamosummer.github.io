I am a third year PhD student at the University of Georgia working with Dr. Jim Leebens Mack & Dr. Tati Russo-Tait. Before pursuing graduate school, I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Botany from Cal Poly Pomona and trained as a post-baccalaureate researcher at Michigan State University. My research explores the evolution of flower color in Geranium, and justice and equity issues in STEM. When I'm not doing academic-related things, I like to roller skate, do ceramics, and visit my local bakery for a hot chocolate and morning roll.
