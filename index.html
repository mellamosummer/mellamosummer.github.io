I am a third year PhD student working with Dr. Jim Leebens Mack & Dr. Tati Russo-Tait. Before pursuing graduate school, I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Botany from Cal Poly Pomona and trained as a post-baccalaureate researcher at Michigan State University. My reserach explores the evolution of flower color in Geranium species, and justice and equity issues in STEM. When I'm not doing academic-related things, I like to roller skate, do ceramics, and visit my local bakery for a hot chocolate and morning roll.
