I am a third year PhD student working with Dr. Jim Leebens Mack & Dr. Tati Russo-Tait. Previously, I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Botany from Cal Poly Pomona and trained as a post-baccalaureate research technician at Michigan State University. I am interested in using computational approaches to understand how flower color changes over time. I also use qualitative methods to explore justice and equity issues in STEM.
